About Me

Hello, my name is John Faulkner and I'm currently a senior at UCF in game design program. I've loved video games as a kid. My first console was the NES, but it wasn't until I got a PlayStation 1 that I played games on a regular basis. I enjoyed games so much that it became more than a hobby, it was my passion. The one game that influence me to go into Game Design was Metroid Prime. It was a game that perfectly balanced art, story, and design to create one of the best experiences I ever had. One day I hope to make games as good as Metroid Prime.

What do I do?

When it comes to video games, I am a artist and storyteller. I am pretty efficient in 2D art, and have some experience with making 3D art in Maya. I also enjoy writing storys for games and developing the characters beyond a steroetypical scope. One day I hope to write a compelling narative to a even more compelling and fun video game.
